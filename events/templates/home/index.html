About Sanskriti

Sanskriti is the National Art ad Cultural festival of Mar Athanasius College of Engineering which is held in three day, 19th the 20th, and 21st of March 2020.
Mar Athanasius college of Engineering rests on a low-lying hillock at a sun-licked spot in Kothamangalam. The ccollege on the hillrocks is a place of mystique, transcending its intriguing history as the first college in Asia under Christian Management, set in stone since 1961.

Jaalakam

As the silver lining of Sanskriti’20, MACE NSS unit presents Jaalakam, the host of a series of events including visits to care homes in the hope to impart the joy of Sanskriti to our special brothers and sisters.

Organized by the student members of MACE NSS Unit and Sanskriti'20 committee, the proposed event aims to draw the attention of the public and media towards the motivation and involvement of the physically challenged community.

As part of Sanskriti’20, Jaalakam has planned a1.5km 'Wheelathon' on 15th March, 2020 in association with All Kerala Wheel Chair Rights Federation. The event is planned to start from Marine drive at 8:00am and will end at Rajendra Maidan. Organized by the student members of MACE NSS Unit and Sanskriti'20 committee, the proposed event aims to draw attention of the public and media towards the motivation of physically challenged community.
